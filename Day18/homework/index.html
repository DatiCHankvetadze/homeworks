Types of IT Services




Managed IT Services:

Ongoing support and management of IT infrastructure and systems by a third-party provider.
This often includes network, server, and device management.



Cloud Services:

Services that provide computing, storage, and networking capabilities over the internet.
Examples include SaaS (Software as a Service), PaaS (Platform as a Service), and IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service).



Network Security Services:

Services focused on protecting an organization's IT infrastructure from cyber threats. This includes firewall management,
intrusion detection, virus and spam blocking, and VPN management.



Data Analytics and Business Intelligence Services:

Services that help businesses collect, manage, and analyze large volumes of data to inform decision-making.



IT Consulting:

Expert advice and guidance on using IT to achieve business objectives. This can include IT strategy development, system implementation, and optimization.



Hardware and Software Support Services:

Technical support for hardware and software issues, including troubleshooting, maintenance, and upgrades.



Disaster Recovery and Backup Services:

Services to protect and recover data and IT infrastructure in the event of a disaster. This includes data backup and restoration solutions.



Communication Services:

Services related to the management and maintenance of communication systems, including email, VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol), and video conferencing.



End-User Support and Help Desk Services:

Support for individual users within an organization, addressing issues related to desktops, laptops, and software applications.



Web Development and Hosting Services:

Services related to the development, maintenance, and hosting of websites and web applications.



Custom Software Development:

Designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining custom software solutions tailored to specific business needs.



Cybersecurity Consulting and Services:

Providing specialized expertise in securing information systems against cyber threats, including risk assessments and security audits.
